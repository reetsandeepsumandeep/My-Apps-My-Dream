Our Story

Welcome to My Apps My Dream - a platform born from the passion to solve real-world problems through technology. It all started when I noticed that content creators were spending hours searching for editing materials across multiple websites. That's when I built Edit Library - an app that brings all editing resources in one place.

Today, we're not just building apps; we're building a community where users can share their ideas and get rewarded for helping others. Every great app starts with identifying a problem, and we believe the best ideas come from the people facing those problems daily.

Our Mission

To create innovative mobile applications that solve everyday problems for millions of users. We focus on quality, user experience, and making technology accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become India's most user-centric app platform where every problem has a digital solution. We envision a community-driven ecosystem where users' ideas shape the future of our apps.
